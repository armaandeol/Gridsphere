Growing Success with Fasal Precision Farming

Story Of The Journey

Once upon a time, in the middle of India's vast farms, Fasal started its journey in January 2018. Imagine a place where old-school farming meets fancy technology - that's where Fasal stepped in as a pioneer in smart farming. How? By using clever things like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Crop Sciences, and the Internet of Things (IoT) to give farmers super-smart info about their crops. This innovative integration empowers farmers with super-smart information about their crops, offering farm-level, crop-specific, and crop-stage-specific intelligence. Fasal's approach enables resource optimization, including water and pesticides, leading to higher farm productivity. Simultaneously, it ensures the procurement of high-quality, traceable produce for an end-to-end optimized value chain play. At its core, Fasal's mission is to empower farmers by solving for guesswork in farming. The full stack offering doesn't stop at data analysis and imparting intelligence for growing better; it takes a step further. Under its B2B brand Fasal Fresh, it procures farm produce to secure the best market rates for farmers while optimising the supply chain play.. Fasal's overarching brand mantra is simple and impactful: 'Grow More, Grow Better, and Sell Better.'

Helping Farmers, One Crop at a Time

We don't just crunch numbers; we go the extra mile. We have this cool brand called "Fasal Fresh" that helps farmers sell their crops at the best prices after they have consumed Fasal's smart advice.

  • Reaching Far and Wide
    We're buddies with farmers in 13+ states, looking after more than 75,000+ acres of land with over 15+ different crops.
  • Saving Water
    We've saved a massive 52+ billion liters of water. That's a lot of water high-fives for being sustainable!
  • Less Pesticides
    We've helped cut down 127,426 kilograms of pesticides. Good for the environment and great for the farmers' wallets.
  • Green Hero
    Our farming tricks have stopped 54,965 metric tonnes of greenhouse gases from floating into the air. High five for being green!
  • Market Magic
    This intelligence propels our B2B brand, 'Fasal Fresh,' procuring sustainably grown, traceable, and high-quality produce.
  • Healthy Treats
    We're connecting farmers to folks in 12 top Indian cities, delivering yummy and healthy fruits.
  • No more Pests
    Our smart predictions mean farmers save money - 18% to 30% less spent on pest sprays.

  • More Crops, More Smiles
